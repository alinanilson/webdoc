Hardware

Recently, the number of incidents connected with attacks on ATMs and self-service terminals of credit institutions that lead to the theft of cash funds has grown substantially.

BlahBlah Cybersecurity offers the following list of works in this area:

  • Remote testing of ATMs through the network (without physical access)
  • The impact on the hardware of ATMs
  • Development of means for exploiting the vulnerabilities of ATMs
  • Demonstration at existing ATMs
  • The development of protection against identified attack vectors
  • Control the elimination of identified vulnerabilities
