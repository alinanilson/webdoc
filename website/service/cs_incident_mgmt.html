CS Incident Management

BlahBlah Cybersecurity helps businesses in the investigation of crimes, the weapon of which was a computer. Our specialists restore the picture of what happened, collecting and analyzing digital evidence from various electronic devices. We help find the perpetrators of the incidents and hold them accountable.

Incident investigation services can include:

  • Collect, systematize and analyze the data needed to investigate the information security incident
  • Exploring the memory images of servers, work computers, mobile devices
  • Analysis of malicious software, definition of the boundaries of defeat
  • Consultation of law enforcement agencies and company support in court
  • Training of personnel in incident response procedures
  • Consulting for the removal of vulnerabilities
